How common is Katakana?

Really common.

Here, I'll prove it to you.

First, try clicking on the "katakana" link over there on the side:

The bits highlighted in yellow are Katakana. After a while, believe it or not, those characters will jump out at your eyes when you look at Japanese text. They have a distinct style about them compared to the other characters used in Japanese.

It is not at all uncommon to see this many Katakana words appear in running text. You can test this theory yourself. Click and drag the "katakana" link on the right to the bookmark bar in your browser, and then go to a random page on the Japanese Wikipedia. (This link goes to a random page on the Japanese Wikipedia.) Then click your new bookmarklet button, and you'll see what I mean.
